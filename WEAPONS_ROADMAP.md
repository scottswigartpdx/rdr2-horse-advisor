# Weapons Feature Roadmap

## V1: Weapons + Upgrades
Core weapon data with base and upgraded stats (similar to horses).

### Categories
- Pistols
- Revolvers
- Repeaters
- Rifles
- Shotguns
- Sniper Rifles
- Bow
- Melee (knife, machete, hatchet, cleaver, etc.)
- Thrown (dynamite, throwing knives, tomahawks, fire bottles)

### Data per weapon
- Base stats: Damage, Range, Fire Rate, Accuracy, Reload
- Max stats (with all upgrades)
- Available upgrades/components (improved rifling, longer barrel, scopes, wraps)
- Acquisition method (store, mission reward, found in world)
- Chapter availability
- Price

### Unique/Rare Variants
Special versions of base weapons with unique stats/appearance:
- Midnight's Pistol
- Calloway's Revolver
- Flaco's Revolver
- Granger's Revolver
- Micah's Revolver
- Otis Miller's Revolver
- Algernon's Revolver
- And others...

## V2: Store-bought Ammo
Ammunition available for purchase at gunsmiths and general stores.

### Ammo types
- Regular
- High Velocity
- Express
- Slug (shotgun)
- Buckshot (shotgun)

### Bow Arrow Types
- Arrow
- Small Game Arrow
- Poison Arrow
- Fire Arrow
- Dynamite Arrow
- Improved Arrow

### Data per ammo
- Compatible weapons
- Effects (damage modifier, range modifier, etc.)
- Price
- Where to buy

---

## V3: Crafted/Special Ammo
Ammunition that must be crafted at campfire.

### Types
- Split Point (all guns)
- Explosive (pistol, rifle, shotgun, arrow)
- Incendiary (shotgun)
- Poison (throwing knife)
- Small Game Arrow
- Improved Arrow

### Data per ammo
- Crafting recipe (ingredients)
- Pamphlet required (if any)
- Effects
