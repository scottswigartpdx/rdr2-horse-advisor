Weapon Gear Guide

Bandoliers, Holsters, Gun Belts & Equipment Sets

The Key Thing to Know

You do NOT have to wear equipment to get the bonuses. Once you purchase gear from the Trapper, the stat boosts are permanent and automatic. Wear whatever looks good.

There are two places to get weapon gear upgrades: the Gunsmith (basic upgrades for cash) and the Trapper (better upgrades from completing challenges). The Trapper's reinforced equipment is superior in every way.

Gunsmith vs Trapper Equipment

Piece Gunsmith Trapper (Challenges)
Bandolier +50% longarm ammo ($69.50) +100% longarm ammo
Gun Belt +50% sidearm ammo +100% sidearm ammo
Holsters Basic + Set bonus when complete

The Gunsmith upgrades are available immediately for cash. The Trapper's reinforced equipment requires completing challenges first, but gives double the ammo bonus plus attribute boosts.

Challenge Equipment Sets

There are 9 challenge categories, each with 10 ranks. Completing challenges unlocks equipment at the Trapper. Each category gives you a full set: Bandolier, Holster, Off-Hand Holster, and Gun Belt.

When you buy all 4 pieces from the same challenge set, you get a permanent attribute bonus (Health, Stamina, or Dead Eye).

Attribute Challenge Sets Bonus
Health Horseman, Sharpshooter, Weapons Expert +1 Health bar per set
Stamina Bandit, Gambler, Master Hunter +1 Stamina bar per set
Dead Eye Explorer, Herbalist, Survivalist +1 Dead Eye bar per set

How the Bonus Bars Work

  • Your natural max level for each attribute is Level 8
  • Complete 1 set in a group → Level 9 (first bonus bar)
  • Complete all 3 sets in a group → Level 10 (second bonus bar)
  • Example: Buy all Horseman gear → Health goes to 9. Then buy all Sharpshooter AND Weapons Expert gear → Health goes to 10.

Recommended Priority

If you want max Health first:

Focus on Horseman challenges (riding/horse skills) - they're fun and natural to complete while playing. Then Sharpshooter (combat challenges) and Weapons Expert (kill challenges).

If you want max Dead Eye first:

Focus on Explorer (finding treasure maps - easy with guides), Herbalist (collecting plants), and Survivalist (crafting/survival skills).

If you want max Stamina first:

Master Hunter (hunting challenges) is straightforward. Bandit (outlaw activities) is fun. Gambler challenges are notoriously frustrating (Blackjack, Dominoes) - save these for last.

Tip: The individual equipment pieces give you the ammo bonuses immediately. You don't need to complete the full set to get +100% ammo - just buy each piece as you unlock it.

The Ultimate Reward

Legend of the East Outfit

Complete all 90 challenges (all 9 categories, rank 10 each) to unlock this legendary outfit with unique bonuses:

-10% Core drain rate
-10% Horse core drain
-10% Dead Eye drain
-10% Damage on horseback
+10% Looting money
+10% XP gain
-10% Weapon degradation
-10% Eagle Eye drain
